CTO & Co-Founder, Akhetonics GmbH

2022 – Present | München, Germany
Founded Akhetonics to develop the world’s first all-optical general-purpose processor. Leading the photonics team. Selected for Intel Ignite and Airbus Combat Cloud accelerators.

Postdoctoral Researcher, Max Planck Institute for the Science of Light

2020 – 2022 | Erlangen, Germany
Focused on nonlinear optics cleanroom fabrication and microresonator-based logic elements. Pioneered work on counterpropagating light in photonic circuits. Supervised multiple PhD and MSc students.

PhD in Applied Photonics, Heriot-Watt University & National Physical Laboratory

2016 – 2020 | United Kingdom
Research on symmetry breaking in Kerr microresonators for all-optical logic, sensors, and gyroscopes. Published in Optica, Optics Express, and Scientific Reports.

MBA Coursework, Edinburgh Business School

2017 – 2019 | Scotland
Courses in Accounting, Marketing, Economics, and Project Management. All grades: A or A+.

BsC and MSc in Matter Physics, University of Florence

2007 – 2015 | Italy
Built laser diode and laser cooling system for experiments on Bose-Einstein condensates of Dyprosium (quantum gases) in quasi-periodic lattices.

Other Highlights

  • Patent co-author of several photonic circuit architectures and devices (Logic gates, optical ADCs).
  • Led over 15 multi-foundry photonic tapeouts on diverse platforms (SiN, InP, etc.).
  • Strong background in microfabrication, circuit design, and Non-linear optics simulation.
  • Reviewer of start-up technology propositions, business models, and papers for Optics Letters, JOSA, and Applied Optics.
  • Speaker and Exhibitor at CLEO, CalBay, and other international optics/tech events.
  • Proposal writer for EU-funded projects totalling over €40M in awarded funding.
